About Us

Meet the team behind Orillia's top-rated nail salon.

Our Story

Meet Emily & Phillip

We're more than just nail technicians — we're a husband-and-wife team who genuinely love what we do. When you visit Pedi N Nails, you're not just another appointment; you're a guest in our salon.

Our clients often tell us they feel like they're catching up with old friends. We take pride in creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where you can truly relax while we pamper you with quality nail care that lasts.

From the moment you walk in, you'll notice the difference — our beautifully decorated space, meticulous attention to cleanliness, and the care we put into every single nail.

What Sets Us Apart

Our Commitment to You

Cleanliness First

Hygiene is our top priority. Our salon is immaculate, and we follow strict sanitization protocols for every tool and surface. You can relax knowing you're in a safe, clean environment.

Quality Over Speed

We never rush. Every nail gets the attention it deserves. We'd rather take our time and do it right than hurry through and compromise on quality.

Results That Last

Our shellac stays chip-free for weeks. Our acrylics don't lift. We use quality products and proper techniques because we want you to love your nails for as long as possible.

Genuine Care

We treat every client like family. Whether it's your first visit or your fiftieth, you'll always feel welcome. We remember your preferences, your favourite colours, and what matters to you.
